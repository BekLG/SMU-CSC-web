St. Mary's University Computer Science Club Terms and Conditions

Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before registering for the St. Mary's University Computer Science Club. By registering, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with these terms.

Mission Statement

The St. Mary's University Computer Science Club aims to provide students with opportunities to explore various aspects of computer science and technology. Through a diverse range of events and activities, including coding projects and hackathons, we strive to foster innovation, collaboration, and community on campus. Our goal is to support academic excellence, research, and professional development in computer science while promoting diversity, inclusivity, and equal opportunities for all members.

Article II: Membership

Section 1: Eligibility

  • The club is open to all students enrolled at St. Mary's University who are studying computer science.
  • No auditions or tryouts are required for membership.

Section 2: Requirements

  • Members are required to attend at least 50% of the club meetings per semester.
  • Prospective members must complete and submit an application form.
  • Failure to meet the member requirements may result in non-membership or forfeiture of all membership benefits.

Section 3: Benefits

  • Members will have access to exclusive events, workshops, and networking opportunities.
  • Special training and internship opportunities may be available to club members.
  • Members can showcase their abilities and skills to the community through club activities.

By registering for the St. Mary's University Computer Science Club, you agree to adhere to these terms and conditions. The club reserves the right to modify these terms as necessary, and any changes will be communicated to members. Failure to comply with these terms may result in disciplinary action, including revocation of membership privileges.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding these terms, please contact the club administration for clarification.
